Our Services

Facility Management

We offer comprehensive facility management solutions tailored to meet the needs of commercial, corporate, government, residential, and industrial clients. Our services span across housekeeping, landscaping, equipment maintenance, and more—ensuring seamless operations and superior cleanliness.

With a structured approach and multiple quality assurance protocols, we perform regular audits and maintain ISO-certified standards. Our front-end and back-end teams work cohesively to deliver smooth, efficient facility operations and labor provisioning.

Housekeeping Services

We recognize that cleanliness and hygiene are critical for productivity and morale. Our trained housekeeping staff ensure your premises are spotless and germ-free. Our services include:

  • Pantry & cafeteria upkeep
  • Cleaning, vacuuming, and scrubbing
  • Chair & sofa shampooing
  • Washroom and common area maintenance
  • Cabin & workstation cleaning
  • Polishing, sanitization, and façade washing

Gardening & Landscaping

We design and maintain both basic and elaborate landscaping tailored to your preference. Our horticulture experts uphold healthy plant growth and proper pruning, maintaining visual appeal and environmental standards.

  • Regular plant maintenance and pruning
  • Asset logs for plant species, condition, and schedule
  • Expert landscaping for indoor and outdoor areas

Carpet & Upholstery Care

We provide deep-cleaning services for carpets and upholstery using eco-friendly solutions that reduce allergens and extend material life.

  • Dust, stain, and wear protection
  • Allergy reduction and odor control
  • Green cleaning products for healthy spaces

Specialized Services

Building Maintenance & Utility Services
