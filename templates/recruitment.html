Operations: Recruitment

Each security personnel at RS Nineteen is chosen with great care and attention to detail. The Internal Security Department rigorously vets every prospective employee by running background checks against them. It is only after qualifying these stages that a candidate becomes a member of the RS Nineteen family.

They have to adhere to stringent parameters which include:
  • Basic Documentation, ESIC & PF Forms
  • Photo ID Check & Residential Verification
  • Previous Employment & Reference Check
  • Medical Check & Mental Health Analysis
  • Exit Interview from Previous Company
  • Discreet Checks: Integrity and Aptitude test
  • Educational & Police Verification
  • Physical Verification: Height and Weight
  • Vaccination Status
