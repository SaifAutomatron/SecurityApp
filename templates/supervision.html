Operations: Supervision

Active On-the-Ground Supervision

To maintain high service standards, we prioritize intensive and proactive on-the-ground supervision. A strong Operations team consisting of Managers, Security Officers, and Patrolling Supervisors conducts around-the-clock inspections across all deployments. Equipped with radio communication and mobile devices, they ensure seamless security management and rapid response.

Site Monitoring

Before onboarding any assignment, our Operations team conducts a comprehensive site inspection to understand security requirements. Based on this survey, we design a tailored security strategy which includes: duties charter, client expectations, emergency protocols, and detailed post instructions—formulated in collaboration with the client.

Client Relations

At Westech, customer satisfaction is a long-standing tradition. We build strong relationships by understanding client needs, anticipating their expectations, and proactively managing concerns. Constructive feedback has helped us prevent potential issues and expand our list of satisfied customers.

Client Feedback

To continuously improve our services, we encourage ongoing client feedback. This system not only enables timely corrective measures but also helps us evolve and align our operations with the highest standards of client satisfaction.
