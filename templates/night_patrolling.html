Our Services

Night Patrolling

Our patrol teams operate round the clock, providing vigilant security across various locations including commercial complexes, construction zones, industrial sites, residential communities, showrooms, and retail stores. This service ensures safety through routine inspections, physical site checks, and rapid response to any alarms or incidents.

Our Patrolling Services Include:

  • Site inspections every 3 hours throughout the night
  • Comprehensive premises checks
  • Monitoring of on-site security personnel
  • Prevention of suspicious activity
  • Radio and line communication support
  • Immediate action in case of emergencies

Dedicated Patrol Supervisors document each visit with detailed reports, supported by photographs of any observed security breaches. Any critical issues are promptly reported to our 24/7 control room.

These incidents may include:

  • Fire or medical emergencies
  • Burglary, theft, or tampering
  • Suspicious vehicles or individuals
  • Irregular sounds or lights
  • Lock status verification
  • Site and material safety concerns
