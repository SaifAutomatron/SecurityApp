Comprehensive training programs tailored for excellence in security services.

Mandatory Security Training

At RS Nineteen, every employee is required to complete a rigorous training program, in line with PSARA guidelines, which mandates a minimum of 160 hours of training prior to deployment. Our training emphasizes essential security skills, including:

  • First Aid
  • Firefighting
  • Self Defence
  • Basic guarding duties

This structured learning path is both progressive and continuous, refreshing core knowledge and enhancing capability throughout an employee’s tenure.

Training Curriculum Includes:

  • Internal Basic Security Training
  • Refresher & Skill Development Programs
  • Client-Specific Specialized Training
  • Security Terminology & SOP Awareness
  • Vigilance, Frisking & Public Surveillance
  • Grooming, Conduct & Communication Skills
  • Gate & Patrol Duties, Equipment Usage

Key Training Areas

  • Physical Training & Drills
  • Access/Gate/Visitor/Material Control
  • Residential Security Training
  • Building & Campus Security Management
  • Evacuation & Disaster Preparedness
  • Fire Safety & Crowd Management
  • Dealing with Protests, Bandhs & Strikes
  • First Aid & Emergency Response
  • Traffic Control
  • Contingency & Incident Handling Plans
